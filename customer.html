Customer Module–E-Commerce Application

The Customer Module is a modern shopping application designed to deliver a seamless online shopping experience, similar to leading platforms in market. It enables users to easily explore products, manage purchases, and complete transactions in a secure and user-friendly environment.

  • Easy Cart & Secure Checkout
  • Wishlist Management
  • Profile & Order Tracking
  • Return & Cancel Options

Customer Module Features

🏠 Home Page

Explore promotional banners, product categories, and discounted items with quick Cart and Wishlist options.

📦 Product Details Page

View product images, pricing, discounts, and variants with easy Add to Cart and Buy Now options.

🛒 Cart Page

Review selected items, update delivery address, choose payment method, and place orders securely.

❤️ Wishlist

Save favorite products and move them to the Cart anytime for quick purchase.

👤 Profile & Orders

Access Wishlist, My Orders, and track order status including Delivered and Cancelled.

📄 Order Options

View order summary, address, payment details, cancel before shipping, or request return after delivery.

🔐 Additional Features

Includes Legal & Policy pages, Contact Us section, and secure Login & Logout functionality.
