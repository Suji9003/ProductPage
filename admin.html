Admin Panel– E-Commerce Management System

The Admin Panel is a powerful and user-friendly management system designed to help business owners efficiently control their online store. It provides secure access, real-time insights, and complete control over products, orders, and customers — all from a single dashboard.

  • Easy Product Upload
  • Order Management
  • Delivery Tracking
  • Sales Reports & Analytics

Admin Panel Features

🔐 Secure Authentication

Admin can log in quickly using “Continue with Google” for secure and fast access.

📊 Dashboard Overview

View, search, filter, and track orders with instant WhatsApp contact.

🗂️ Category Management

Add, edit, delete, and organize categories and subcategories easily.

🛍️ Product Management

Manage products, pricing, offers, and images with simple editing options.

📦 Orders Management

Track, search, update order status, and contact customers via WhatsApp.

👥 Customer Management

View customer details and connect instantly through WhatsApp.

⚙️ Settings & Configuration

Update profile, manage store info, customize homepage, and logout securely.
